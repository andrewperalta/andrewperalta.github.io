A B O U T

I’m Andrew Peralta, an MBA-trained Senior Game Designer with a background in software development and a deep focus on user experience. Currently, I lead live-ops and systems design for Sniper Fury at Gameloft Hanoi—driving engagement, retention, and revenue growth through feature design, progression systems, and data-driven iteration.

Over the years, I’ve shipped multiple titles across casual, mid-core, and casino genres, combining player psychology, F2P economy design, and UX strategy to deliver sustainable live games. I’m passionate about how systems, events, and content loops can tell stories, motivate players, and grow communities.

Guided by the values of excellence, integrity, grit, and empathy—I approach every design with both creative curiosity and business awareness.
